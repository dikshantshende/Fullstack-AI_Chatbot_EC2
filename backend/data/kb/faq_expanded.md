# FAQ Expanded

## Returns & Refunds
- We offer a 30-day return window. Items must be unused and in original packaging.
- Refunds processed within 5–7 business days after inspection. For expedited refunds, contact support.

## Shipping & Delivery
- Standard shipping: 5–7 business days. Express: 1–2 business days (fee applies).
- Delays due to carrier: allow up to 10 business days before reporting lost packages.
- Track using your order ID on the support portal.

## Warranty & Repairs
- Electronics: 12-month limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.
- For repairs, contact our support to generate an RMA number.

## Damaged Items
- If item arrived damaged, we can issue an exchange or refund after receiving photos and a support ticket.

## Order Issues
- To check an order status, ask: 'Check status of order ORD101' or 'Where is my order ORD104?'
- To create a support ticket: 'Create a ticket for ORD103 | item arrived damaged'
